Claire Modersitzki About Me

There is power in writing your story, and from a young age, I always wanted Disney to be a part of mine. From the catchy songs to the details down Main Street USA, everything speaks when interacting with The Walt Disney Company. My personal story to this point has a variety of different chapters; the learning phases, the celebratory phases, and the heartbreak phases, but if I have learned anything when it comes to a good story is that the page always turns. I am anxious to add a chapter of the magic to my personal story. Disney is intentional with the story it delivers, and it caters to all readers regardless of skill level, age, or demographic. It is my invitation that you write me into your story, and however that looks regardless of the speaking part I am given, to be a part of the Disney story would be a long-awaited and anticipated turn of the page.  Let's grab a pen...and start writing! Claire E. Modersitzki
